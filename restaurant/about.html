CrossOver is our place name and we named it after our menu that is a little bit mixed between italian food and japanese food. It started small.At first it was just our preferences for foods, a small food truck and our passion for cooking.

We had a dream about becoming the best restaurant in the country and we had to give our best to accomplish that dream.

We were 8 people who worked so hard and sacrifice so much for the better good.
After 5 years of hard work, we opened our first restaurant and here we are on the top list of restaurants, we are growing more than ever and we are trying our best to treat our guests with respect and passion, the same passion we had when we started and still have until this day.
